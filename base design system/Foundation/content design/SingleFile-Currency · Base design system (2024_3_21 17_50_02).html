                                                                                            Currency

                                                                                            Formatting for the same currency can be different for users who have different language settings on their phones. One person’s app may say EUR 10 while another’s says €10. The upshot? It’s important to know the guidelines for handling currency and to apply them correctly, but some aspects are out of our control.


                                                                                            The examples here are given in US currency, but the guidelines apply to all currency everywhere.

                                                                                            TL;DR

                                                                                            • To express a monetary value (in US dollars and all other currencies), use the currency symbol — don’t spell it out — and the numerals

                                                                                            • Separate values in a range with an en dash, with spaces before and after

                                                                                            • In a range, use the currency symbol in front of both numbers

                                                                                            • Values in a range don’t need to be expressed to the same number of decimal places

                                                                                            • Express values in a list (for instance, a receipt) to the same number of decimal places so the numbers align properly

                                                                                            • For whole numbers, use trailing zeros only when it's important to indicate the number is exact, usually for values like earnings and prices

                                                                                            Expressing monetary values

                                                                                            Don’t spell out values

                                                                                            Always use currency symbols and numerals to express amounts, including in headers and body text, even at the beginning of a sentence. It’s preferable to writing longer text, and it’s more scannable.

                                                                                            Do

                                                                                            Don't

                                                                                            $30 added to your Uber Cash balance

                                                                                            Ten dollars added to your Uber Cash balance

                                                                                            He earned $45.25 for that delivery


                                                                                            The rider tipped 8 dollars last time

                                                                                            Whole values

                                                                                            • Use the dollar sign ($) before the amount

                                                                                            • In body text, for whole amounts (with no cents), don’t include a decimal or trailing zeros

                                                                                            Do

                                                                                            Don't

                                                                                            $25

                                                                                            $20.00

                                                                                            $19.99

                                                                                            20 dollars

                                                                                            $125,000

                                                                                            $100K

                                                                                            Values less than $1

                                                                                            Use a zero (0), a decimal point, and the cent value to 2 decimal places.

                                                                                            Do

                                                                                            Don't

                                                                                            $0.15

                                                                                            15¢

                                                                                            $0.10

                                                                                            10 cents

                                                                                            Zero value 

                                                                                            Use a zero (0) rather than spelling out zero.

                                                                                            $0, not free

                                                                                            • When a fee is waived for some reason, like a special promotion, don’t refer to it as being free. Instead, say it’s $0  

                                                                                            Do

                                                                                            Don't

                                                                                            Special promotion delivery fee: $0

                                                                                            Special promotion delivery fee: free

                                                                                            $0 delivery fee

                                                                                            Free delivery

                                                                                            • For other incentives where there’s no specific value attached, it’s OK to use free

                                                                                            Do

                                                                                            Get 5 free ride credits when you refer 3 friends.

                                                                                            Negative values 

                                                                                            Use a minus sign (hyphen) to indicate a negative number. 

                                                                                            Do

                                                                                            -$25

                                                                                            -$0.99

                                                                                            Monetary ranges

                                                                                            • Use the currency symbol before each value in the range, and separate the values with an en dash (–), with no spaces before and after

                                                                                            To insert an en dash, press Option + hyphen on a Mac keyboard.

                                                                                            Do

                                                                                            Don't

                                                                                            $25–$50

                                                                                            $25 – $50

                                                                                            $12–$14.50

                                                                                            $12–14.50

                                                                                            US$35.50–$40

                                                                                            US$35.50–40

                                                                                            • It's OK to mix whole and partial values. Don’t add trailing zeros to a whole number just to match the decimal spaces of another value in the range

                                                                                            Do

                                                                                            Don't

                                                                                            $10–$14.75

                                                                                            $10.00–$14.75


                                                                                            Itemized lists 

                                                                                            Any place where dollar amounts appear in a list, use trailing zeros to keep the decimals aligned properly, for scannability and comprehension. 

                                                                                            It’s rare for things like earnings or prices — values that show in receipts — to be whole values. Adding the trailing zeros makes it clear that the values shown are exact.
