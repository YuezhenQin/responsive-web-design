                                                                                                                                Messaging

                                                                                                                                Tooltip

                                                                                                                                Tooltips are brief, informative messages used to introduce new content and features or provide brief step-by-step guidance.


                                                                                                                                Tooltips usually appear for a short amount of time or in some cases, until the user dismisses them.

                                                                                                                                Usage
                                                                                                                                Content
                                                                                                                                Spec
                                                                                                                                Changelog

                                                                                                                                Writing guidelines

                                                                                                                                Content

                                                                                                                                Tooltip messages must be short and easy to understand in the short time a tooltip appears.

                                                                                                                                • Be brief. Keep content to 1-2 lines (3 lines maximum). See additional guidance in the character length section below.

                                                                                                                                • Don't convey crucial information. Don’t use tooltips for legal information or any other content that a user may need regular access to.

                                                                                                                                • Use simple language. It's OK to use phrases or short sentences, with simple verb forms to make the content easy to understand at a glance.

                                                                                                                                • Avoid simply labeling the UI. Good tooltips are more about discovery, revealing something a user might not learn on their own. Content should focus on what a feature helps the user accomplish, or highlight what needs the user’s attention.

                                                                                                                                • Don't convey status updates and feedback: There are other components better for communicating this type of information.

                                                                                                                                Do

                                                                                                                                Don't

                                                                                                                                Tooltip pointing to an account icon:

                                                                                                                                New invoice available


                                                                                                                                Billing information

                                                                                                                                Tooltip pointing to a profile icon:

                                                                                                                                Tap to switch the profile


                                                                                                                                Profile

                                                                                                                                Tooltip pointing to user's delivery address:

                                                                                                                                You’re a little far away


                                                                                                                                Delivery address

                                                                                                                                Punctuation

                                                                                                                                • For a single-sentence tooltip, don’t use final punctuation

                                                                                                                                • For a multi-sentence tooltip, use a period at the end of each sentence

                                                                                                                                • Never use a question mark or exclamation point, since tooltips are meant to be directive, functional, and instructive

                                                                                                                                Character length

                                                                                                                                Mobile
                                                                                                                                Add notes

                                                                                                                                Tooltips on mobile should take up no more than 50% of the screen width, or a 10 m length.

                                                                                                                                Web
                                                                                                                                Add notes

                                                                                                                                Tooltips on the desktop should take up no more than 50% of the content grid width, or a 20 m length.
