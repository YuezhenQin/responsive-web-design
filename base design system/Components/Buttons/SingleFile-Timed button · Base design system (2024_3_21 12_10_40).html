                                                                                                                                Timed button

                                                                                                                                Stable

                                                                                                                                Timed buttons are used as a variation of our rectangle buttons. The main difference is that, unlike standard ones, timed buttons auto-advance the user after a finite amount of time (usually a few seconds).
