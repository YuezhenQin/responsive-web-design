                                                                                                                                Progress steps

                                                                                                                                Stable

                                                                                                                                A progress steps component is a series of milestones that provides an overview of a complex process or multistep flow, like a transportation itinerary or account creation flow. Progress steps help guide and encourage users as they use our apps.

                                                                                                                                TalkBack

                                                                                                                                In most cases, each list item in a list isn’t considered a button. Rather, TextViews are nested inside of a RecyclerView. Click events are given to the TextViews, which is why they aren’t actually buttons in this case.
