                                                                                                                                Tabs

                                                                                                                                Stable

                                                                                                                                Tabs are a navigational component that allows users to move easily between groups of related content.

                                                                                                                                Usage
                                                                                                                                Specs
                                                                                                                                Status & changelog

                                                                                                                                Metrics

                                                                                                                                Fixed-width tabs

                                                                                                                                Fixed-width tabs are defined by the container's width divided by the number of tabs. Minimum padding of 16 will be maintained. If the text of a single tab doesn't fit, the intrinsic-width tabs will be used instead.


                                                                                                                                Intrinsic-width tabs

                                                                                                                                Intrinsic-width tabs are defined by the width of their label (and icon), plus 16 padding on either side. These tabs can extend past their container width and will be scrollable

                                                                                                                                Wide viewports

                                                                                                                                A leading margin is added for wider viewports to compensate for larger content margins. The line will be extended to the full width of the container
